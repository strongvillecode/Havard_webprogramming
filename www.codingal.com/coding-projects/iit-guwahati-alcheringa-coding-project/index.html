  • Points increase with the difficulty level of the problems.
  • Real-time leaderboard.
  • The student with the highest points will be the winner.

Competition Schedule

  • Competition Date: 5 Nov, 2023
  • Competition Time: 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM IST
  • Results and Felicitation ceremony on 15 Nov, 2023

Judging

  • The problems will be partially graded, which means, you will be awarded points for passing each test case.
  • There are no penalties for incorrect submissions.
  • Feel free to submit as many solutions as you like; amongst all your correct submissions, the best code will be considered. .

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create my Coding Project?

To begin, simply register on our website using the provided link. Once registered, you will be assigned a dedicated mentor who will guide you throughout the journey of creating your project.

What is the program fee?

After completing the free mentorship session, our academic counselor will provide you with detailed information regarding the fee structure. The program fee starts at $250 for a 24-hour live online class.

How long is the program?

The program consists of a 24-hour live online mentorship session spread across 8 weeks.

When can I submit my coding project?

The deadline for project submissions is October 31, 2023.

Is the 24-hour program mandatory to receive the IIT Guwahati Alcheringa certificate?

Yes, in order to be eligible for the certificate, you must enroll in the 24-hour online program curated by IIT Guwahati Alcheringa. However, please note that you can still create and submit your project to participate in the contest and be eligible for prizes without taking the 24-hour program. However, in this case, you will not qualify for the certificate.

Who will judge the coding projects submitted by students?

We have computer science experts who have been empanelled to judge the projects

How will the coding project be judged?

The coding project will be judged on 5 criteria - Originality, Uniqueness, User Interface, Cleanliness & Quality of Code

Why should I register for this program?

This program has been carefully curated by IIT Guwahati, a prestigious technology university ranked 6th in the NIRF in India. Registering for this program grants you access to invaluable expertise, guidance and an opportunity to acquire multiple skills. Upon completion, you will receive a certificate from IIT Guwahati Alcheringa.

Our mission and vision
