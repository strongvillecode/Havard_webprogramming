What do we do?
We are a champion team of IIT, Harvard, NYU, and IIM alumni, as well as Ph.D holders and ISRO scientists providing 1:1 live classes to children aimed at arming them with the power of technology creation and mathematical thinking. We have our own proprietary teaching platforms apart from the ones from Google, MIT, Autodesk, and others.
What topics are covered in the BrightCHAMPS curriculum?
The BrightCHAMPS curriculum covers a range of topics in a fun-based and interactive manner. In addition to coding and robotics, they also offer financial literacy classes to help children develop sound money management skills. Language classes are also offered to help children learn new languages and improve communication skills.
How does the BrightCHAMPS curriculum help kids perform better academically?
The skills and methodologies taught by BrightCHAMPS are designed to complement the school curriculum and can help children perform better in their regular studies. By using coding, robotics, financial literacy, and language learning, children can develop problem-solving skills, creativity, and critical thinking abilities. The curriculum also aims to instill confidence in children, encouraging them to take on challenges and pursue their interests.
How do we select kids for our programs?
At our organization, we select the best-suited program for your child based on their developmental needs, interests, strengths, and challenges. We offer a range of programs, including CodeCHAMPS for coding, FinCHAMPS for financial literacy and entrepreneurship, RoboCHAMPS for robotics and AI, and LingoCHAMPS for communication and public speaking. Our team of experts provides personalized attention to each child and regularly updates parents on their child's progress. We are committed to helping children discover their passion and reach their full potential.
