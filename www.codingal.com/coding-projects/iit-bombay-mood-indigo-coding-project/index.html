Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create my Coding Project?

To begin, simply register on our website using the provided link. Once registered, you will be assigned a dedicated mentor who will guide you throughout the journey of creating your project.

When can I submit my coding project?

You can submit your project anytime before 11:59 PM, January 31, 2024.

Who will judge the coding projects submitted by students?

We have computer science experts who have been empanelled to judge the projects

How will the coding project be judged?

The coding project will be judged on 5 criteria - Originality, Uniqueness, User Interface, Cleanliness & Quality of Code

Why should I register for this program?

This program has been curated by IIT Bombay Mood Indigo, a prestigious technology university, ranking 4th in the NIRF rankings in India. Participating in this program grants you access to invaluable expertise, guidance, and an opportunity to acquire in-demand skills. Upon completion, you will receive a certificate fom IIT Bombay Mood Indigo.

Our mission and vision

Codingal is on a mission to inspire school kids to fall in love with coding. Coding is proven to develop creativity, logical thinking and problem solving skills in kids. Coding is an essential skill of the future and more than 60% of all jobs in STEM-related fields require knowledge of code. Kids who start learning to code at a young age are guaranteed to become leaders, creators and entrepreneurs of the future.
